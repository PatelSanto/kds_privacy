Frequently Asked Questions

🔐 I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login screen, tap "Reset Password" and enter your registered email address. You will receive a password reset link shortly. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact your HR Admin.

📅 How do I apply for leave?

Log in to the app → Go to Quick Access → Tap "Apply Leave" → Fill in the leave type, dates, and reason → Submit. Your Team Leader or HR Admin will be notified for approval.

✅ How do I mark my attendance?

Go to Quick Access → Tap "Mark Attendance". Attendance can be marked based on your organization's configured method (location-based or manual check-in).

📊 Where can I view my payslip?

Navigate to Payroll from the Quick Access menu. Your monthly payslips are available for download as PDFs.

👤 How do I update my profile information?

Tap your profile icon at the top right → Select "View Profile" → Tap Edit to update your details. Some fields may require HR Admin approval to change.

🔔 I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?

Go to your device Settings → Notifications → KDS HRMS and ensure notifications are enabled. Also check that you have a stable internet connection.

🗑️ How do I request deletion of my data?

Email us at kdsservices25@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" along with your registered email and employee ID. We will process your request within 7 business days.

📱 Which devices and iOS versions are supported?

KDS HRMS supports iPhone running iOS 15 and above. For the best experience, we recommend keeping your device updated to the latest iOS version.
